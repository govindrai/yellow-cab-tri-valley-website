The Original, Trusted Yellow Cab

Dependable. Affordable. Since 1938.

Since 1938, Yellow Cab Tri-Valley has been providing professional service and quality vehicles to residents and visitors in the East Bay area. With competitive rates, on-time service, and friendly drivers, we offer you quality service you can trust.

Whether you are an individual rider, a small business or a large corporation, you can expect great, responsive service from us. We want your business and we will do our best to earn it and keep it.

A Word From the CEO

We are the tri-valley’s largest, best-equipped taxi service. Several generations of riders can attest to the safe, efficient and courteous service we provide. Our drivers are trained and experienced and our vehicles are clean and well-maintained.

Yellow Cab Tri-Valley has always been family-owned and operated. In 2003, I started driving for the company as a weekend gig. I fell in love with the business, the customer-service aspect,  and the rush in picking people up and getting them safely to their destinations! In mid 2005, I had the fortunate opportunity to take over the business.

This business is my bread and butter and I make it my duty to ensure we deliver you the best service. Give us a ring, (just remember all 9's!) and let us serve you!

-Rakesh Rai, CEO
